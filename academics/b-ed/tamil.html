Empowering future Tamil educators with professional pedagogical skills and Design Thinking methodologies.

Course Overview

About the Course

The Pedagogy of Tamil in the B.Ed. programme focuses on the systematic study of teaching Tamil as a first language. It aims to develop a deep understanding of Tamil literature, grammar, and linguistics while mastering innovative teaching techniques.

Trainees learn to create an immersive language learning environment, integrating Design Thinking to solve classroom challenges and enhance student engagement with the Tamil language.

Curriculum Structure

  • Aims and Objectives of Teaching Tamil
  • History of Tamil Language and Literature
  • Micro-teaching and Lesson Planning
  • Teaching Language Skills (LSRW) in Tamil
  • Assessment and Evaluation in Language Teaching

Teaching Methodology

Our innovative approach combines traditional language teaching with modern pedagogical frameworks.

Design Thinking

Applying empathy-based instruction to solve complex language learning barriers.

ICT Integration

Using digital tools and multimedia content to teach Tamil literature and grammar.

Peer Teaching

Collaborative learning sessions to refine micro-teaching and instructional skills.

Skills Developed

  • Advanced Tamil Literacy

    Proficiency in classical and modern Tamil prose and poetry.

  • Instructional Designing

    Capability to draft effective lesson plans and teaching aids.

  • Classroom Management

    Mastering the art of maintaining child-centric learning environments.
