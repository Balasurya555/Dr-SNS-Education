Course Overview

About the Course

The Pedagogy of Mathematics focuses on the various methods and techniques of teaching mathematical concepts to school students. Trainees learn to simplify complex theories, algebraic equations, and geometric proofs using modern pedagogical frameworks.

The course integrates logical reasoning and critical thinking, employing Design Thinking to help students overcome Math Anxiety and develop a love for numbers through empathy-based instruction.

Curriculum Structure

  • Nature, Scope, and Values of Teaching Mathematics
  • Historical Development of Mathematical Concepts
  • Micro-teaching and Instructional Planning in Math
  • Methods: Analytic, Synthetic, Inductive, and Deductive
  • Evaluation and Diagnostic Testing in Mathematics
